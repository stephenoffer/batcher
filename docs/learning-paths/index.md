# Learning paths

Role-based reading guides through the documentation. Each path is an ordered list of
pages plus a couple of small runnable examples, so you can follow it top to bottom
and arrive at the parts of Batcher that matter for your work.

- [Data engineer](data-engineer.md): pipelines, joins, aggregations, reading and
  writing data.
- [Data scientist](data-scientist.md): interactive analysis with expressions, SQL,
  and aggregations.
- [ML engineer](ml-engineer.md): batch inference, embeddings, and GPU execution.
- [Platform engineer](platform-engineer.md): configuration, resources, and
  operating the engine.
